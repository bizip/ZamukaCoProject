    Zinnia, Forecast

    The 'Forecast' is for zinnias all summer! ‘Forecast’ is a long-lasting zinnia that will bloom endlessly and is tenacious enough to thrive in virtually any conditions. Its mildew resistance makes it the obvious choice for cut flowers in damp, cool areas like the Pacific Northwest and Canada. It bears purple, pink, orange, salmon, yellow or cream petals that surround bright-yellow stamens for a 2-3" across bloom. No matter the weather, our ‘Forecast’ is for gorgeous zinnias—all summer long.

    Zinnia, Forecast

    Exceptionally long lasting blooms on tall, sturdy upright stems, this eye-catching mix will not only bring long-lasting colour to the garden throughout the summer months, but their cut-and-come-again nature means you can fill your vases with these beautiful blooms without losing your outdoor display! Zinnia elegans ‘Forecast’ is a mixture of single and double flowers in an exotic mixture of purple, pink, orange, salmon, yellow and cream shades. Drought tolerant, ideal for a hot, sunny spot and also a great choice for attracting pollinators like bees and butterflies to the garden. Height: 60-80cm (24-31”). Spread: 30cm (12”).
